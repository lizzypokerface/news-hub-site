### Hyper-Imperialism: A Dangerous Decadent New Stage

![Hyper-Imperialism: A Dangerous Decadent New Stage](https://thetricontinental.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Covers-e1706025205302.jpg)

In 'Hyper-Imperialism: A Dangerous Decadent New Stage,' a groundbreaking study by the Tricontinental Research Institute, the decline of Global North hegemony is examined alongside the rise of organizations in the Global South, signaling a seismic shift in geopolitics. Delving into the complexities and opportunities of this transition, the study underscores the risks inherent in this new era of global power dynamics.
